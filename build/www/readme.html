BonziWORLD Independent

Leading the industry in gorilla-based chat clients.

What the hell is this?

BonziWORLD Independent is a node.js and socket.io based chat client featuring everyone's most ~~hated~~ loved purple gorilla. The client also uses speak.js to provide text-to-speech voices. Not exactly the original BonziBUDDY voice, but hey, it works.

How do I use it?

  1. Enter a nickname (under 25 characters). If you don't enter one, you'll be named "Anonymous".
  2. Optionally, enter a room ID.
  3. Be a BonziBUDDY.

What are the chat commands?

  • /name [name] - Change your name.
  • /color [color] - Change your BonziBUDDY's color! The ones available are:
    • red
    • brown
    • green
    • blue
    • purple
    • black
    • pink
    • glitch
    • code
    If you don't type a color, you will be given one at random.
